How to Use the Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get an accurate estimate of your monthly carbon footprint.

Step 1: Home Energy

  • Enter your monthly electricity usage in kilowatt-hours (kWh). You can find this on your electricity bill.
  • If you use piped natural gas, enter the amount in Standard Cubic Meters (SCM) from your gas bill.
  • Add any other fuel sources you use, like Kerosene, LPG cylinders, or wood.
  • If you generate solar power, enter the amount to have it subtracted from your total.

Step 2: Transportation

  • Estimate the total kilometers (km) you travel per month for each mode of transport.
  • Include your personal car, public buses, trains or metro, and any flights you've taken.

Step 3: Consumption & Diet

  • Estimate the amount of different food types your household consumes in a month, measured in kilograms (kg).
  • Foods like red meat (mutton) have a much higher impact than plant-based foods.
  • For "Non-Food Shopping," estimate the number of new items (like clothes, electronics, etc.) you buy in a typical month.
